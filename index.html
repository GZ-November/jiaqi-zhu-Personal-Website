About Me

I am Jiaqi Zhu, a student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) pursuing a BSc in Risk Management & Business Intelligence (RMBI), Class of 2029.

I care deeply about how industries work — supply chains, competitive dynamics, and the macro forces shaping them. My lens combines data analysis, financial modeling, and cross-market research to understand not just what's happening, but why it matters.

I currently serve as a Research Analyst at Mortise Capital, where I produce weekly macro recaps tracking global market rotations, sector-level moves, and policy signals. I also consult as a Business Analyst for XinJiang Tianyun Organic Agriculture, applying supply-chain diagnostics and market entry frameworks to real-world decisions.

I'm still learning — and I think that's the point. Every report, every project, every conversation teaches me something new about how the pieces fit together. This site is where I share the work in progress.
